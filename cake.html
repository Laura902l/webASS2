Best-Ever Chocolate Cake

It's a blend of luxurious cocoa powder, creamy Hellman's mayonnaise, and an exceptionally moist texture that simply dissolves on your palate. Every slice of this cake is like an enchanting dive into the realm of pure chocolate delight. This delightful dessert combines the depth of rich cocoa with the velvety richness of Hellman's mayonnaise, resulting in a cake that offers a unforgettable flavor experience.

Ingredients

FOR CAKE

Directions

Set a Timer
Step 1

Make cake: Heat oven to 350˚F. Coat 9-by-13-in. pan with nonstick spray; line with parchment, leaving overhang on 2 long sides; spray parchment.

Step 2

In large bowl, whisk flour, both sugars, cocoa powder, baking soda and salt until no clumps remain. In second bowl, whisk warm water, Hellmann’s mayonnaise, vanilla and espresso powder until smooth. Make well in dry ingredients and gradually add liquid mixture, whisking constantly until batter is smooth.

Step 3

Transfer batter to prepared pan, smooth top and bake until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean 42 min; then, using parchment overhangs, transfer cake to wire rack to cool completely.

Step 4

Meanwhile, make frosting: Using mixer on medium, beat butter until smooth. Reduce speed to low; add confectioners’ sugar, vanilla and salt and mix to combine. Increase speed to medium and beat until smooth and creamy, then beat in milk. Spread frosting over cooled cake and sprinkle with chocolate chips.

Gingerbread Bundt Cake

This cake embodies the flawless blend of comforting, aromatic spices, soft and delicate pastry, and a sugary glaze that dissolves effortlessly on your taste buds. Its distinct flavor is a source of delight and solace, suitable for any occasion. Elevate your get-togethers with loved ones by introducing this exceptional cake, turning ordinary moments into extraordinary memories.

Ingredients

FOR CAKE

    • GLAZE

Directions

Set a Timer
Step 1

Make cake: Heat oven to 350°F. Grease 91/2-inch (10-cup) Bundt pan with melted butter, then sprinkle with granulated sugar, tapping to coat, making sure to get into all ridges.

Step 2

In medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and spices.

Step 3

In stand mixer, beat butter, granulated sugar, and grated ginger on medium-high until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs 1 at a time, scraping bowl after each addition. Beat in vanilla.

Step 4

In 4-cup measuring cup or medium bowl, stir molasses into 1 cup hot water. With mixer on low, add flour mixture to butter mixture in 3 increments, alternating with molasses mixture, until just combined..

Step 5

Pour batter into prepared pan and smooth top. Bake until toothpick inserted into center comes out clean, 45 minutes. then, using parchment overhangs, transfer cake to wire Let cool in pan on wire rack 20 minutes. Using small offset spatula, gently loosen edges and invert onto rack. Let cool completely.

Step 6

Meanwhile, make glaze: In medium bowl, whisk together confectioners’ sugar, sour cream, heavy cream, vanilla, and salt, adding more confectioners’ sugar or heavy cream if needed, until thick and pourable.

Step 7

Pour glaze over cake and garnish with pomegranate arils and freshly cracked black pepper.
